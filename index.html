The Synapse Conference

The Mental Health Conference brings together experts, advocates, and individuals to explore the latest research, treatments, and strategies for fostering mental well-being. Through insightful discussions and workshops, participants gain valuable insights into innovative approaches, destigmatizing conversations, and promoting resilience, ensuring a collective commitment to mental health awareness and support.

Explore The Modern Means of Mental Health

Neuroscience and mental health are relevant and important topics to the younger population. However, besides the brief talks surrounding mental health at school and in general, many young individuals don't know much about their brain, how it works, and why they feel certain ways. The Synapse Conference was created to tackle this problem and to further educate students on these relevant topics. By brining in high level speakers, the conference aims to share leading research regarding neuroscience and further break down stigmas surrounding mental health.
